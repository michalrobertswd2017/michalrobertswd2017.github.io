Welcome to Rainbow Play Therapy! We are a london based therapy clinic who provide play therapy sessions for children. Very often a child may be going through a difficult phase, showing modes of behaviour that can be concerning. We are proffessionals who will work with your child and through various sessions will work with your child to find a solution to his/her issue. No issue is too big or small for us. We have experience dealing with all kinds of behavioural issues in children.
